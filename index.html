About Section

MISSION

Sacred Heart Hospital provides up-to-date; cost-efficient diagnostic, therapeutic and complementary medical services to its patients and their families.

We seek to participate in nation-building by making a significant contribution to atain and maintain the health of the province of Bulacan, Region III and ultimately the nation.

Our philisophy of competent, compassionate and efficient service is grounded on serving the interests each patient and their families as our allies in providing health to all.

VISION

To achieve this, we regard capacity building as our responsibility to develop our capability to serve bearing in mind that our most valueable resource is our staff.

Part of this is a judicious use of technology as a tool to complement competence.

Lastly, we believe in sustainable growth as an institution. Our ability to thrive as a business ensures our ability to achieve this mission.
